What the trial looks like.

The free pilot is designed to produce real outcome data with zero financial risk to the practice.

Weeks 2–12: Your front desk works from the ranked list. Every outreach, booking, and fitting is logged in the dashboard.

Day 90: We present the data — flagged vs. unflagged conversion rates, incremental revenue estimate, and a recommendation.
